How it works

Step 1

Tell us your health goals

Share your health objectives, and we'll suggest appropriate health challenges for you. Alternatively, you can select one from our Mission library to get started!

Step 2

Carry out missions

Engage in your preferred daily healthy activities within each
health Mission, we'll support you throughout your journey!

Step 3

TRAC your progress

Monitor the TRACback points you accumulate by meeting your daily mission goals using the TRACring. Don't worry if you miss a day, each new day presents an opportunity to make progress.
