Our Story

Our Journey


TRAC was founded in 1998 and headquartered in Singapore.

TRAC first set foot in the aviation industry with its cutting-edge GPS navigation products.

Over the last 2 decades, we have expanded our product lines to encompass the aviation, marine, and automotive sectors.

Our Mission


Today, TRAC is the renowned leader in the aviation, marine, automotive, outdoor, and fitness markets.

Based on a robust product ecosystem, captivating designs, unwavering quality, and exceptional user experiences, TRAC aspires to be the brand of choice for you.

Focus on Health and Wellness


We are inspired by individuals who share our passion for embracing life to the fullest. At the heart of TRAC's mission is the belief that everyone should have the opportunity to lead healthier and more active lives.

It is our commitment to health and wellness that led us to design an exceptional range of products.

Connecting with Fitness Enthusiasts


Our diverse collection of watches is tailored to meet the unique needs of fitness enthusiasts and those on a journey towards a healthier lifestyle.

Whether you're an avid runner, a dedicated cyclist, a passionate hiker, or someone simply committed to achieving better health and losing weight, TRAC offers a companion on your path to wellness.

Empowering Your Journey


With cutting-edge technology, precision and style, our watches are engineered to monitor your progress, provide insights and motivate you to reach new heights. We're not just timepieces. We empower individuals to live their lives to the fullest.

Our Founders

Alex Voss

Rajan Menon

TRAC was founded in 1998 by the visionary minds of European entrepreneur Alex Voss and Singaporean innovator Rajan Menon. Alex, from Munich, Germany, graduated from the Technical University of Munich, while Rajan earned his Aeronautical Engineering degree from the National University of Singapore.

TRAC initially entered the aviation industry with ground-breaking GPS navigation products. Inspired by this encounter, they pioneered the integration of GPS into compact navigation devices, a breakthrough that gained widespread popularity.

TRAC's journey mirrored the evolving landscape of wearable technology, with its initial focus on aviation, marine, automotive, outdoor, and fitness sectors. The name "TRAC" represents the device's inherent ability to track, which corresponds perfectly with its role in guiding you on your health and fitness journey.
