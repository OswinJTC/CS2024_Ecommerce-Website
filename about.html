We are TRAC.

Welcome to TRAC, your trusted destination for watches in the heart of Singapore.
We are passionate about enhancing the world of wearable technology and fitness tracking, and we're on a mission to redefine the way you experience health and fitness.

Our Journey and Commitment


TRAC was founded in 1989 and headquartered in the United States.

TRAC first set foot in the aviation industry with its cutting-edge GPS navigation products almost three decades ago.

Over the years, we've expanded our product lines to encompass the aviation, marine, and automotive sectors.

Our Mission to Provide You with the Best


Today, TRAC is the renowned leader in the aviation, marine, automotive, outdoor, and fitness markets.

Based on a robust product ecosystem, captivating designs, unwavering quality, and exceptional user experiences, TRAC aspires to be the brand of choice for individuals who savour every moment of life.

A Focus on Health and Wellness


As we reflect on our journey, we are inspired by the individuals who share our passion for embracing life to the fullest. At the heart of TRAC's mission is the belief that everyone should have the opportunity to lead healthier, more active lives.

It's our commitment to health and wellness that has led us to introduce an exceptional range of products.

Connecting with Fitness Enthusiasts


Our diverse collection of watches is tailored to meet the unique needs of fitness enthusiasts and those on a journey towards a healthier lifestyle.

Whether you're an avid runner, a dedicated cyclist, a passionate hiker, or someone simply committed to achieving better health, TRAC offers a companion on your path to wellness.

Empowering Your Journey


TRAC watches aren't just timepieces; they're your partners in achieving your health and fitness goals.

With cutting-edge technology, precision, and style, our watches are engineered to monitor your progress, provide insights, and motivate you to reach new heights. We're not just about selling watches; we're about empowering individuals to live their lives to the fullest.

Join the TRAC Community

At TRAC, we invite you to become a part of our community of individuals who value their health, wellness, and active lifestyles.
